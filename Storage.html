Storage

Within the ambitious plan of SMARG HERBS, the storage phase is considered one of the important and sensitive stages in the production process. we depend on the warehouse department team to develop the appropriate strategy for each product separately. Specially with the large quantities of raw materials and finished products, we mainly depend on the system of separation between warehouses and the use of plastic pallets in storage and take into account the appropriate temperature and humidity to maintain the physical and chemical properties of the products.

We also guarantee our customers a product that is free from any insects or pests, and with full physical and chemical characteristics, by automatically tracking the product from raw materials to packaging.

We are so pleased to receive our customers to follow the entire production process and extremely proud of all the products offered to the world that match global prescriptions and quality.
